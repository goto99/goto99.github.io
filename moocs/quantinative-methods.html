Week 1 Origins of Scientific Method

Scientific method

  • empirically testable, by observations.
  • replicable
  • objective, under clear assumptions, concepts and procedures
  • transparent
  • falsifiable, or contradiction made possible.
  • logical consistent, hypothesis internally coherent, and conclusion logically consistent.
